Cascade Retail Group (SYM007) — Q1 2026 Earnings Call Transcript

Operator: Welcome to Cascade Retail Group's first quarter 2026 results call.

CEO remarks: This was a very challenging quarter. Comparable sales declined 9% as consumer traffic
weakened and promotional pressure intensified. We took a significant inventory write-down and are
lowering our full-year outlook. Margins compressed sharply and we are disappointed with execution
in our core segment. We are cutting costs and pausing new store openings amid an uncertain demand
environment.

CFO remarks: Cash flow was negative in the quarter and we drew on our revolving credit facility.
We are suspending the dividend to preserve liquidity while we work through elevated inventory.

Analyst Q&A: Analysts expressed concern about the deteriorating margin profile and weak guidance.
Management acknowledged the difficult backdrop and declined to commit to a recovery timeline.
